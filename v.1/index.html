Peaches

Peaches are juicy, sweet stone fruits with a fuzzy or smooth skin and a pit in the center.

Peaches have a round shape with a cleft on one side. Their flesh can be white, yellow, or red, and their skin can be fuzzy or smooth. Peaches continue to ripen after they have been picked. Unripe fruit can be ripened at room temperature, and once ripe, should be refrigerated to prolong freshness.
